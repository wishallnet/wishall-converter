Chanakya Font Converter

1. The Gold Standard of Print

Chanakya is known for its beautiful, artistic curves. It is the primary font for major Hindi newspapers like Dainik Bhaskar and Rajasthan Patrika.

2. Why Chanakya breaks in other tools?

Chanakya uses "Ligatures" (joined characters). For example, 'Kra' (क्र) in Chanakya is a single unique symbol, not a combination of 'K' + 'Ra'. Other converters treat these as errors or question marks (??).

3. The Wishall Advantage

Wishall maps over 500+ rare Chanakya glyphs. We handle "Halant" (half-characters) properly so words like 'Vidya' (विद्या) stay joined and beautiful, just like in print.
